Our Story

Welcome to our enchanting world of candles, where artistry meets pop culture! At Lit Candle Studio, we are passionate about transforming your space into a realm of warmth, nostalgia, and style. Step into a realm where flickering flames dance to the tune of your favorite movies, TV shows, and iconic characters.

Lit Candle Studio was founded in 2020, during the Covid-19 Pandemic. Each candle we create is a labor of love and are hand-poured in small batches from our Melbourne Studio.

OUR WAX

Our candles are made with high quality soy wax that is sustainably sourced. Our unique soy blend is biodegradable and vegan friendly. No animal products are used and no animal testing has been carried out in its manufacture.
